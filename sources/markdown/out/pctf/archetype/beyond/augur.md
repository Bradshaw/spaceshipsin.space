# Augur

Augurs learn to tune their senses to the Beyond, providing insight into, and protection from, paranormal phenomena.

## Special equipment: Critterbox
*Beyond Resist +2 / Beyond Resist +1 for nearby allies*
The skeleton of a small animal, often a mouse or bird, encased in lensing resin and placed into a sealed wooden box. It senses and redirect threats to the attuned Augur, allowing them to focus their mind elsewhere.

## Special ability: Scatter Walk
*Cost: Take damage*
When the Administrator is describing a scene or situation, you can flash back to a moment in the past where your mind scattered in the beyond and prepared you for this moment. This cannot undo or change any already stated facts. You didn't not necessarily know *why* you made the preparations in question. You might not even remember having done so. Yet you did.

## Remnant: The Shakes
*Once per investigation*
Make a statement to the Administrator. A short fact that you believe might be true.
The Administrator must either confirm or deny.
If they confirm, your next action roll that is related to this fact is bolstered.
If they deny, you take damage.
If they are unable to confirm nor deny, you take damage, but you may use this ability again.
