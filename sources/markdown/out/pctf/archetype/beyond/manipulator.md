# Manipulator

Manipulators are trained in the art of projecting their will into or through the Beyond. They are taught how to shape paranormal phenomena and act within the Beyond in relative safety.

## Special equipment: Stabilising harness
*Beyond Force +2 / Beyond Move +2*
A harness worn under your uniform, tethering you to realspace while you let your abilities wander

## Special ability: Idle Hands
*Cost starts at one stress, and increases by one each use*
Automatically succeed on an action to make something impossible happen in your immediate vicinity
*e.g:*
    - Walk through a solid wall
    - Open a locked door
    - Change your appearence

## Remnant: Patching the holes
*Once per investigation*
Take one stress and automatically succeed on an action to block paranormal phenomena from occuring in a small area around yourself for a up to one minute. You many move, and the area of effect follows you.
