# Investigator

The purest form of investigation work. Observe, detect, dig into the mess, search for the needle in the haystack. Investigators are the eyes and ears of the PCTF.

## Special equipment: Anchored Notebook
*+1 Percieve*

## Special ability: Para-linguistic programming
When in conversation with an NPC, if you are able to get them to pronounce one of the following words, you may attempt to induce a program within them. The induction word must not have been pronounced in the conversation yet, and it may not be written anywhere the NPC can see or may have seen recently. The NPC cannot be forced or coerced into using the induction word. While they may be under duress, the use of the induction word itself must be or their own accord.
Induction words:
- System / Procedure / Process / Technique / Program / Schedule
- Denial / Rebuttal / Debunk / Rejection / Reject / Negative / Negate
- Fragment / Remnant / Splinter / Shatter / Fracture / Crack / Fissure
- Unseen / Lurk / Lurking / Invisible / Undetected / Concealed
- Doubt / Dread / Terror / Horror / Unease / Unrest / Turmoil
- Faith / Trust / Hope / Confident / Confidence / Pray / Prayer

To induce a program, explain how you project it into their mind, and make an appropriate beyond action roll.
Regardless of the outcome, you may give them a simple order, or ask them a simple question with a one word reply. As long as the order or question does not put them in direct danger, they must comply.
- Fiasco / Messy: Take damage
- Critical: They stay under your control for another order or question in the minutes that follow.

## Remnant: The Shakes
*Once per investigation*
Make a statement to the Administrator. A short fact that you believe might be true.
The Administrator must either confirm or deny.
If they confirm, your next action roll that is related to this fact is bolstered.
If they deny, you take damage.
If they are unable to confirm nor deny, you take damage, but you may use this ability again.
